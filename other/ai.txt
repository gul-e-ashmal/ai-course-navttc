Python for AI, not for web fluff

Master NumPy, Pandas, and Matplotlib/Seaborn for data manipulation and visualization. Stop “just importing”; actually understand broadcasting, indexing, and vectorized operations.

Learn PyTorch deeply (or TensorFlow if you insist). Not just tutorials—understand tensors, gradients, autograd, and how models update weights. This is the engine of AI.

Understand how to structure AI experiments: training loops, evaluation metrics, overfitting, underfitting.
